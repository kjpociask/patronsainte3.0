About Patron Sainte

Patron Sainte helps people live life as their most extraordinary selves by supporting their entrepreneurial spirit through brand strategy, UI/UX design, culture consulting and business strategy.

Patron Sainte was founded by Kate Pociask (pronounced like 'Poe-chask'). Kate is a creative director and culture consultant for agencies and startups. She also does digital product design, meaning anything that has a screen and users. Kate has worked in design for 10 years, 5 of those spent as an entrepreneur, and 5 before that holding roles like Creative Director and Art Director at boutique agencies.

Trained In

  • User Interface/User Experience Design (UI/UX)
  • Human-centered Design ? (the IDEO/Design Kit Method )
  • Design Thinking ? (the IDEO Method)

Special Skills

As a professional, Kate has been recognized for her inquisitiveness, ability to see potential in people, and ability to see the big picture. She speaks and writes about the relationship between creativity, productivity, psychology, and mental health. She speaks English and French.

As a Human

In her spare time, Kate volunteers as a dog walker, trainer and photographer at the Wisconsin Humane Society. She dabbles in several languages. In 2018, Kate, her partner and their dog lived in France for 3 months, where they uprooted their life as a cultural experiement. Their australian shepherd/border collie came with them, picking up a little French as well.
